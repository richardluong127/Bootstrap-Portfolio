About Me

I have been experiencing the front end result of many hours of collaborative effort by many developers on many digital projects. Over two decades in, I am indeed a consumer of digital content.

As memory serves, I recall that day many years ago when I went home for the first time wearing a contraption comprised of steel clamps fitted around my molars which a steel apparatus could slide into on each side: one part of it housed in my mouth while the other portruded out around my cheeks and connected to a strap with adjustable resistance which went around my neck. They called it a neckbrace and it was part of my ortho prework to help fix my overbite, still I knew it as the bane of my 5th grade as far as perceptions went. The nagging pain and the bars which actually were a bit too long at first causing it to tear into the back of my mouth was a thing.

As it turns out, during that same time I also received a handme down NES with some video games and decided to try it out. All of a sudden the pain became much lighter and I noticed my focus shifted away from the brace and onto what was happening right in front of me.

I was told it would change, but it looks like throughout all the years I was the one who turned out to be the constant :).
